Who we are

We build automation systems your team can rely on, year after year.

Averon Automation builds PLC, HMI and SCADA systems for manufacturing and warehousing operations. We work across Mitsubishi, Delta, Siemens, Allen-Bradley and Schneider hardware, and bring plant-wide visibility through Zenon and FT Optics SCADA — choosing whichever combination suits the process, the budget and the team that has to run it after we leave.
